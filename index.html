About Us

Founded in 2024, India Roleplay Community is a community dedicated to ERL:LC private server. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where members can share their interests, enjoy, and have fun.

How to Join

Joining our Discord server is easy! Just follow these steps:

  1. Click on the invite link: Join Us on Discord
  2. If you don’t have a Discord account, create one for free.
  3. Once you’re in, verify yourself and start your RP with IRP!
