About Us

The India Roleplay Community (ER:LC) is a Discord community server based on the ER:LC private server. Enjoy playing IRP!

Our History

The India Roleplay Community (ER:LC) was founded on July 18, 2024, to create a welcoming community for ER:LC roleplayers. Our founding team, Shubhra9143Q (Founder), 0003ASHIR (Former Co-Founder), and Andy (Former Co-Founder), have worked hard to create a safe and fun environment for all players. Now, we have over 100 members and we are trying to get to 500 members! #RoadTo500
