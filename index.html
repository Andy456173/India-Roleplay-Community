Our History

The India Roleplay Community (ER:LC) was founded in July 18, 2024 to create a welcoming community for ER:LC roleplayers. Our foundership team Shubhra9143Q (Founder), 0003ASHIR (Former Co-Founder) and Andy (Co-Founder) have worked hard to create a safe and fun environment for all players.
